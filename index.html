About Us

‘London Paris’ multiplex provides the ultimate movie-watching experience through an advanced Dolby Digital sound system, best-in-class projectors and 3D systems, comfortable reclining seats with ample leg space and a variety of munchies. The two-screen premium plex is owned and operated by Mukti Group and is located in the boutique mall ‘Mukti World’ in the posh area of Ballygunge in the heart of South Kolkata. The theatre is an extremely popular entertainment destination among the premium residents of South Kolkata and several corporate who love the personalised service and smart movie scheduling.

Multiplex on-screen advertisement is one of the hottest and fastest growing media for branding and advertising. It has several advantages:

  • Captive Audience - audience cannot switch off the ad unlike while watching TV at home
  • High Impact - ads are displayed in a dark theatre on a large screen and cannot be missed once inside the auditorium
  • Excellent Audio-Visual quality - ads are played through the best technologyprojectors and sound system
  • Higher Attention Span - eyes are always towards the screen and sitting posture cannot be changed, customers are bound to see the entire ad
  • Exclusivity - only one ad is visible at a time unlike road-side hoardings / newspaperads
