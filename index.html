About Us

‘London Paris’ multiplex provides the ultimate movie-watching experience through an advanced Dolby Digital sound system, best-in-class projectors and 3D systems, comfortable reclining seats with ample leg space and a variety of munchies.

Advertise with Us

Multiplex on-screen advertisement is one of the hottest and fastest growing media for branding and advertising. It has several advantages:

  • Captive Audience - audience cannot switch off the ad unlike while watching TV at home
  • High Impact - ads are displayed in a dark theatre on a large screen and cannot be missed once inside the auditorium
  • Excellent Audio-Visual quality - ads are played through the best technologyprojectors and sound system
  • Higher Attention Span - eyes are always towards the screen and sitting posture cannot be changed, customers are bound to see the entire ad
  • Exclusivity - only one ad is visible at a time unlike road-side hoardings / newspaper ads
